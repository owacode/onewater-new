About OneWater Xchange

As the name suggests, the OneWater Xchange platform is a state-of-the-art learning management system that truly engages the learner and the subject matter expert. The OneWater Xchange platform is designed specifically for the OneWater community in mind with an emphasis on working professionals so that you could plan your learning to fit your schedule.

The value proposition of the OneWater Xchange platform includes microlearning, succession planning, ask the expert, tracking of continuing education credits, and gamification. The most loved and powerful feature of this platform is what is called Discover, Coach, and Share. This feature allows the younger water professionals to ask any questions from a dedicated resource pool of subject matter experts all while collaboratively learning and engaging through the social learning module. Experiences and interactions in the field can be shared with other peers while passively growing the learning experience and knowledge capacity.
